BRETAS SOCCER INTERNATIONAL

Our head office is in London and we also have an office in Rio De Janeiro, Brazil. Our network provides opportunity for our clients movement and onward careers BSI believes in a “Total Management” approach when it comes to football: identifying talent, investing in and developing young players and assisting them every step of the way, offering advice and guidance to help them make the best possible career and business decisions both on and off the pitch and the company’s goal is to breathe new life into the market with its unrivalled work ethic and global outlook to the beautiful game. Our company works globally with very talented players who have the right support and structure from day one with our committed and very professional management team.
